Controls:

  • Movement: WASD
  • A: L
  • B: Comma
  • L: Shift
  • Z: K
  • Start: Spacebar
  • C-stick: Arrow Keys

Keyboard controls listed on the left. There also is controller support. Highly recommended for touchscreen devices.
